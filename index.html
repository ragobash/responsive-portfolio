About Me

Hey there! My name is Ryan Harris and I am a current student in the Houston Coding Boot Camp offered by UT Austin. I'm learning all about frontend and backend development, as well as database management.

My interests in regards to coding, as they stand right now, is investigating the intersection between art and technology, as well as being UI/UX work. With my love for design, the user's experience and the actual client-facing side of development seems to be right up my alley!

I'm excited to learn everything that the boot camp has to teach me! Even though I know it's going to be difficult, I know that it will be worth it Ever since I heard Donald Glover talk about his passion for coding, it ignited the same fire in me.
